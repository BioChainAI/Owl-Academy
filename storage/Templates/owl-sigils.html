Header Sigils

The academy's signature header pair. The Left Sigil (Mathematical Manifold) and the Right Sigil (Archive Cipher) are reusable, dependency-free SVG + CSS snippets — the same markup that lives in the Learning Hub, the Home page, and the Grand Archives.

Left Sigil

Mathematical Manifold

Rotating 24-stream manifold, hexagram, and 7-point star around a counter-rotating Chi-Rho. Used at the top-left of the Home, Learning Hub, and Grand Archives headers.

Right Sigil

Archive Cipher

A spinning twin-triangle frame around a static Chi-Rho. Used at the top-right as a "return" link (wrap it in an <a>).

How to use

  1. Copy the SIGIL ANIMATIONS <style> block (top of this file) into your page once.
  2. Copy the LEFT and/or RIGHT sigil <svg> into your header.
  3. Keep every gradient id unique per page (e.g. goldGradLeft, goldGradRight) — duplicate ids make the browser apply only the first gradient.
  4. Tailwind is optional. Only the three CSS classes (spin-slow, spin-slow-reverse, pulse-glow) are required; sizing is whatever wrapper you give the SVG.

Typical header placement (absolute corners over a centered title):

<header class="relative ...">
  <div class="absolute top-4 left-4 w-20 h-20 md:w-28 md:h-28 pointer-events-none">
    <!-- LEFT SIGIL svg -->
  </div>
  <a href="..." class="absolute top-4 right-4 w-20 h-20 md:w-28 md:h-28">
    <!-- RIGHT SIGIL svg -->
  </a>
  <h1>OWL ACADEMY</h1>
</header>
