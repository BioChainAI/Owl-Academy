## Overview

Sacred Geometry & Topology is the foundational department of Owl Academy. It bridges rigorous mathematics with the metaphysical architecture of space — exploring how structure, proportion, and continuity govern the geometry of the universe at every scale.

The department is organized into five curriculum branches, each approaching dimensional geometry from a distinct angle.
