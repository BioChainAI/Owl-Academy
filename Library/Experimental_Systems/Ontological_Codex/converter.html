Two channels, both recovered: text from the key stream (token ids — exact, independent of precision); the geometric chain from the quaternion deltas. The default lossy packet (Form 0-2) decays over the chain; Form 3's corrective offset reconstructs it losslessly. TTMPT crystallization is avalanche-like, so the deltas are high-entropy — the outer scale is lossless, middle/inner are SLERP-approximate. Hashing doesn't create compressibility; the keys keep the text exact regardless.
