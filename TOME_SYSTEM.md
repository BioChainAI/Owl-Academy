# Owl Academy — Tome & Artifact Mastery System

## Overview

The Tome System is the core progression mechanic of Owl Academy. Knowledge is measured in **Tomes** — discrete units of mastered content — which crystallise into **Artifacts** upon collection. Artifacts are the keys to the sealed departments.

The full path comprises **145 Tomes** across ten departments, producing **11 Artifacts** and unlocking the **Consciousness Triad** (Departments VII, VIII, IX).

---

## Tome Status Definitions

| Status | Tag | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Active Tome** | Colored (dept palette) | Full lesson content exists. Completing it awards XP and counts toward Artifact progress. |
| **Manuscript** | Amber `Manuscript` | Outline and planned sections are published; body content is in progress. Readable but awards **no XP** until complete. |
| **Reference** | Cyan `Reference` | Cross-department resource (manual, tool, reference doc). Readable at any time; awards **no XP**. |
| **Sealed** | Grey `Sealed` | Department is locked pending prerequisites. Inaccessible until unlock conditions are met. |

---

## The Artifact System

Nine tomes within a domain → 1 Artifact. Artifacts are the collectible proof of mastery.

```
9 Tomes → Artifact (Departments I–IX)
16 Tomes → Artifact (Department X only)
```

Each artifact represents a complete conceptual cluster. You cannot earn an Artifact by partially completing its tome set.

## XP & Progression Rules

1. A Tome must be **fully read and assessed** (quiz/sandbox completion) to award XP.
2. Manuscripts award **0 XP** until their `Active` publication date.
3. Reference documents award **0 XP** but may be required reading cited within active tomes.
4. Artifacts are awarded **automatically** when all tomes in their domain cluster are marked complete in Firestore.
5. The **Dept X prerequisite check** runs on login: if all 4 Substrate Artifacts are present in `user.artifacts`, Departments VII–IX unlock globally for that user.
6. XP carries over; there is no XP decay or expiry.
