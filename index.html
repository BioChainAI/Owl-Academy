Optimal way to explore & learn

New here? Follow the path of the Acolyte.

  1. Forge your Cosmological ID. Visit the Registrar to seal a Genesis identity — it signs everything you do.
  2. Open the Control Panel. The Learning Hub is your dashboard for progress, departments, and tools.
  3. Choose a Department Hall. Start in Department I (Foundations) — or jump to any department.
  4. Mint a Minor Tome. In the Seal Forge, mint your personal seal (your signing pen).
  5. Request a course unlock. From a Hall or the Major Tome Library, request access — an Instructor combines seals to unlock it.
  6. Study the tome and its lessons, proofs, and sandboxes.
  7. Claim completion. When finished, claim it — an Instructor or Archon validates and signs it off.
  8. Collect Artifacts & ascend. Nine tomes crystallize into an Artifact; Department X's four Artifacts unlock the Consciousness Triad (VII–IX).
